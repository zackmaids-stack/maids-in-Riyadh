# Maids in Riyadh - Professional Cleaning Service Website

A modern, responsive website for a professional maid cleaning service in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Service Showcase**: Display 6 different cleaning services with descriptions
- **Pricing Plans**: Three-tier pricing structure (Basic, Standard, Premium)
- **Testimonials**: Client reviews and ratings
- **Contact Form**: Easy booking system with WhatsApp integration
- **Smooth Animations**: Professional animations and transitions
- **Modern UI**: Clean and professional design with brand colors

## Services Offered

1. Residential Cleaning
2. Commercial Cleaning
3. Deep Cleaning
4. Move In/Out Cleaning
5. Window & Glass Cleaning
6. Upholstery Cleaning

## Pricing (in Saudi Riyals)

- **Basic Package**: 150 SAR/hour - General cleaning services
- **Standard Package**: 250 SAR/visit - Comprehensive cleaning
- **Premium Package**: 400 SAR/visit - Full deep cleaning with extras
